Kalman filtering

It should be noted that this performance was achieved only after a Kalman Filter was used to smooth the BSI and rFBE time series. Without this smoothing, AUC values of 0.84 and 0.87 were found for BSI and rFBE respectively. Applying a Kalman filter is therefore essential for accurate asymmetry detection because it results in a relative performance increase of 41% for BSI and 26% for rFBE.

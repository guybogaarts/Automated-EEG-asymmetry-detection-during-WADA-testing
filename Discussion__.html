Discussion

Quantitative EEG analysis provides continuous and objective indicators of anesthetic effects and could be used to decide when to perform memory recall and language tests (Tu 2015). This is task is usually performed by a clinical
neuropsychologist. Therefore, the annotations provided by the clinical neuropsychologist online during the Wada tests are as golden standard. Hence, quantitative EEG based asymmetry detection is optimized with respect to the clinical neuropsychologist's annotations.
